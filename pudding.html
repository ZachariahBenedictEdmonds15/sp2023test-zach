Pudding.

Pudding is the first thing that came to mind for things I could flim myself doing, so I decided to do it. It's pretty simple to do as well, considering that the instructions given basically boil down to:

  1. Mix Powder and Milk.
    • Per every packet being used, add 3 cups of milk. It's a lot of milk.
  2. Cook the Pudding.
    • Do this at medium heat.
    • Consistently stir.
    • When the pudding starts acting like pudding, it's done, shove it into the fridge.
